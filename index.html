FAQ

What is Headquarters?

Headquarters is a self-hosted command center for AI coding agents. It lets multiple AI models (Claude, Codex, DeepSeek, and more) coordinate on tasks, share a task board, schedule prompts, and request your approval before taking risky actions.

How is this different from a single AI coding assistant?

A single assistant works alone. Headquarters gives you a team: a supervisor agent breaks goals into tasks, specialized agents execute them in parallel, and you approve the results. It's an AI workforce, not a pair programmer.

Do I need my own AI subscriptions?

Yes. Headquarters connects to your existing Claude, Codex, or other API keys. You bring the models — we provide the coordination layer.

Is my data private?

Completely. Headquarters is self-hosted on your machine. No data ever leaves your infrastructure. Your API keys, task history, and agent conversations stay local.
